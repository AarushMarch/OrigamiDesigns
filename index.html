Origami is the act of folding paper to make crazy things like dragons, boats and even swans!

So stop reading and get folding!

  • In Japan, origami has inspired a lot of art, fashion, food, and even architecture!
  • There are more than a dozen National Origami Associations throughout the world!
  • Akira Yoshizawa is considered the Grand Master of modern Origami!
  • The fastest time to make 100 origami cranes is 40 minutes 35 seconds!
  • Origami is derived from two Japanese words: Ori (folded) and Kami (paper).
